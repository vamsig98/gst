Who Have to File GST Returns?

Business owners who have completed their GST registration have to return the GST files according to their business.

GST Return Filing Advantages

  • Unified Platform:By implementing GST, all the indirect taxes are standardized. Business owners no need to maintain multiple VAT registrations under GST in different states. Single GST registration is enough for sale and purchase goods across India.
  • Subsuming of Taxes: Central Sales Tax, Luxury Tax, Purchase Tax, etc. comes under subsuming taxes. But now, all the taxes are subsumed and made into single tax under GST.
  • Ease of Doing Business: Many businesses have to comply with VAT as well as Service Tax regulations which create the compliance burden on business. But under GST, all the variations are gone between the goods and services.
  • Lower taxes: In some states, businesses have to comply with VAT when they cross their annual turnover of Rs. 5 lakhs. Buts GST liability accrues when they cross an annual turnover of Rs. 20 lakhs in India and Rs. 10 lakhs in the northeast.

GST return is a document which consists all the information of taxpayers sales, purchase, tax paid for your purchases (input tax), and collect tax on sales (output tax). GstGola is the leading GST service provider in India from GST registration to return filing.

How to File GST Returns ?

  • GSTR - 1: Monthly Details of outward supplies of GST. Taxpayers have to file the GST Return by the 10th of every following month.
  • GSTR - 2: Monthly Details of inward supplies and it has to be filed the GST Return by 15th of every following month.
  • GSTR - 3: Monthly GST Return file with the payment of Tax and it has to be filed by on or before 20th of next month.
  • GSTR - 9: Annual GST Return Filing which has to be paid by 31st of December of every financial year.
Who does GST Return Filing ?

Business owners who have registered under GST have to return the GST files according to their business. Even Nil GST return has to be filed under GST return.

What happens if the GST return is not filed on time ?

If the taxpayer has not filed their GST return within the due date, the taxpayer has to pay the late fee of Rs. 100 per day up to Rs. 5000.

What is the Periodicity of GST Filing Return ?

Periodicity has specified based on the type of registration and transactions. Foreign Non-Residents, Tax Deductors, and E-commerce Operators, Input Service Distributor, Regular taxpayers have to file the monthly GST returns.

Can I pay the due taxes after filing the GST Return ?

No, the returns will be invalid if the taxpayer has not paid the tax before filing the GST return.

What type of GST returns need to be filed when a registered business terminates its operations permanently ?

If any business terminates permanently, all the operations of that business need to file the GSTR-10 which is also known as Final GST Return.
