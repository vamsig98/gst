What Do You Mean By GST Registration?

Business whose turn over is greater than 20 lakh rupees are mandatory to register under GST. This process is known as GST Registration. And the GST Registration is mandatory for some businesses. This is mandatory for all the entities involving in the buying and selling or else providing the services in India. The duration of the GST Registration is hard for 2 to 6 days. GST Registration can be done in various cities like Bengaluru, Mumbai, Chennai and New Delhi.


Who all need to Register with GST?

  • Individuals who registered under the Pre-GST Law like VAT, Excise, Service Tax.
  • Having the annual income above the threshold limit of Rs.20 lakhs.
  • The Casual Taxable Person and Non-Residential Taxable Persons who donâ€™t have fixed business place.
  • Supplying for other states.
  • Supply of Goods and Services through e-commerce operator.
  • Taxpayers under reverse charge mechanism.
  • Supply of online information for the people living in other countries, other than the registered taxable person.
  • The agents of the supplier and the input service distributor.

Required Documents for GST Registration Process

Individuals who all need to register under GST must have the following documents.

  • PAN card of the Applicant.
  • Aadhaar Card.
  • Identity Proof or Address proof of the directors with photographs.
  • Bank Statement
  • Digital Signature
  • Authorization Letter for Signatory.
  • Address Proof for Business Place.
  • Business Required Document.
Who are exempted from the GST Registration ?

The GST Registration is not mandatory for the people like:

  • Who supply agriculture products from the cultivation.
  • Makes non-taxable supply of goods and services.
  • Making supplies under the reverse charge mechanism
Who is a casual taxable person ?

Persons who have registered the business in India and having the supplies from other states in India without a fixed place of business, Such persons are known as Casual Taxable Persons.

What do you mean by ARN in GST Registration ?

ARN refers to the Application Reference Number which is proof after the submission of the application to the GST servers. And this is generated by the Temporary Reference Number (TRN) and uploading of the documents.

Can we register for GST voluntarily ?

Yes, entities claiming the input tax credit can register voluntarily, even if it is not liable to register.

Registration granted for any person is permanent or not ?

Once the registration is granted, it is permanent unless it is canceled, suspended.
