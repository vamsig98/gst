Role of GSP And ASP

GSTN introduced the GSPs (Goods and Service Tax Suvidha Provider) which is a unique concept used to cover over the millions of users or taxpayers for performing all the functionalities easily. ASP (Application Service Provider) focuses on all the information of taxpayerâ€™s sales and purchases and converts them into GST returns.


With the help of the GSP and ASP services, Users or Taxpayers can file their returns. Taxpayers have to pay some money for filing returns under GSP and ASP. Taxpayers or users can file their returns under GSP and ASP easily without the knowledge of taxation and Taxpayer have just to fill the essential details, and it automatically calculates the returns and submits to the GST portal. GSP and ASP save the time and money for the taxpayers.

GSP and ASP are treated as a third-party application that offers the convenient method to the taxpayers to interact with the GST systems, uploading invoice details, from registration to filing returns.

Things to Remember while Choosing GSP/ ASP

  • Data Protection and Privacy: Make sure that the GSP/ ASP can maintain the data security strengths, data privacy, and the arrangement of ASP to protect the shared data.
  • Value Added Services: Check whether it is providing Value Added Services like tax alerts, trend analysis, and legal support.
  • Data Retrieval & Audit Trail: It is important to check the data retrieval which is used to record preservation of the taxpayer.
What are GSPs and how do they help with GST ?

GSPs or GSP providers are authorized by the GSTN (Goods and Service Tax Network). GSPs encourage the people to upload the GST returns safely in the prescribed format.

What will be their role ASPs and who are they ?

ASP defined as Application Service Providers. ASP is very helpful for the taxpayers to plan the GST returns as monthly and yearly wise. It enables the reconciliation of returns appropriately and timely credits.

Why do you need GSP Services ?
  • Uploading of Invoice on GSTN
  • Downloading of data of inward supplies
  • Reconciliation of Input Tax Credit
  • Accessing GST Ledgers
  • Uploading of GST Returns
Will GSPs have the commercial arrangement of Goods and Service Tax Network ?

  • The GSTN agreement includes an arrangement to be signed with the GSPs and GSTN
