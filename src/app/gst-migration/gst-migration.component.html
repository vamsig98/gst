Easy GST Migration Process

Have you not yet migrated to GST? Then do it right now. GST Migration is the first step to complete by the every business owner. The existing taxpayers must soon register under GST. Individual taxpayers registered under VAT/Service/Tax must enroll on the common portal by validating the email address, mobile number. Or else by directly through facilitation center which is notified by the commissioner. On completion of the provisional registration, the provisional\ registration must be converted into final registration.


Steps for GST Migration Process

To Migrate to GST, the individual taxpayers must have to follow the below migration steps.

  • Firstly create the user ID and password on the common portal.
  • Enter your business details.
  • Enter the details of the Proprietor, Manager, Director, and Partner.
  • Fill the details of the Authorized Signature.
  • Details of the Principal Place of Business, Additional Place of Business.
  • Enter the details of the Supplied Goods and Services.
  • Also, enter the Bank Account details.
  • And the last step is the final verification.

Eligibility for Migration to GST

Taxpayers are responsible for registering under the GST System. Therefore the individuals registered with the following authorities are eligible for the GST Migration process.

  • VAT
  • Service Tax
  • Central Excise
  • Entertainment Tax
  • Luxury Tax
  • Entry Tax
Is GST Migration and Registration is different in case of GST ?

Yes, Migration and Registration are two different processes under GST. And this Migration is the first step in the GST Registration.

Who is required to Migrate or Enroll under GST ?

If you are the existing dealer under VAT, then you need to migrate/enroll under the GST.

Does GST Migration/Enrollment is mandatory for the Taxpayers ?

Yes, Migration to GST is mandatory for the taxpayers under the GST Law.

What happens if I do not update my GSTIN ?

One should update the GST IN or else you will be not able to take advantage of credits on the tax we collect.

Is there is a fee for enrolling in the GST system portal ?

No there is no fee or charge levied on the taxpayer for the enrollment on the GST System portal.
