ERP Purchase helps you to manage various vendors, supplier’s contacts; you can track your purchases, and also get the entire purchase history, etc. It records the purchase requests, orders, returns, various suppliers, etc. which enables you to know the product delivery time, quality, cost, etc. ERP Purchase automates the process to identify the various suppliers, purchase order to the supplier, price, as well as billing process.



From our GstGola, you are allowed to check the statistics of the supplier’s performance through their reporting like discounts given on the products, quantities purchased, delivery delays, etc. And from here, you can make the sales orders, share products, supplier’s customers, and also maintain the invoices for companies.

ERP is a leading business which helps you to handle the deliveries and that allows you to track the items of your purchase. GstGola also gives the price list of the items, so that you can make your decision smarter. Our integrated purchase management solution gives you the simplified interface to make the best decisions on purchase.

Purchase Reports and Invoice
