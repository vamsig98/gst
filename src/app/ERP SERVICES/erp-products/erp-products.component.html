Features of Our ERP Products

Look at the below features given by the GstGola product management.

  • Product Control: We improve the product or stock tracking through the updated stock levels. Stock levels are updated automatically when the sales and purchases are made. GstGola maintains all your products in multiple warehouses with multiple currencies.
  • Purchasing: We create, modify, and email the purchase orders. And you are allowed to check the reports of the orders.
  • Order Management: We increase your business efficiency by synchronizing your orders. You can integrate the orders and access the reports of sales in real time.
  • Order fulfillment: You are allowed to control your orders at a lower price and in a greater volume. And you can integrate your channels, locations, etc.
  • Product Reports: We give the accurate product reports in real time. Our product maintenance service allows you to run the reorder reports of the stock, etc.
  • Sales Reports: GstGola gives you the accurate reports on sales which give the insights to make better decisions on your business. We also allow you to generate the history and order reports of sales by product, customer, location, channel, and many more.
  • Price List: Maintaining the price list of the product is important to run your business efficiently. GstGola allows you to maintain the custom price list for customer types, various currencies, categories of the products, etc.
  • Mobile App: GstGola provides a mobile application to the Android and iOS users where you can maintain the customers, products of your orders.
