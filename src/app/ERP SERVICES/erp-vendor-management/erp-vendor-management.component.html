GstGola vendor management automates the interaction with the supplier and reduces the manual errors. We often keep on tracking the order status without interacting with the vendors or suppliers. Our Vendor management helps in reducing the time consumption on handling the vendor queries and in making the work easier.

Irrespective of the number of vendors and the size of the organization or the company, we GstGola helps you in managing the vendors. We also keep on updating the vendor records of the payable accounts.


Features of Vendor Management

  • Adding vendors to the system: Here we maintain the details of the vendor such as opening balance, credit terms and as well as the financial transactions.
  • Flexibility: It has the flexibility to modify the vendor details like contact information, vendor category, preferred delivery mode, credit terms, and payment.
  • Vendors List in Export Formats: Effortless exporting of the vendor lists in the convenient file formats such as PDF, CSV and etc.
