We GstGola will take care of all your expenses and keeps track of the records as well.GstGola Expenses ERP Service helps in simplifying and accomplishing the expenses reporting. We offer various advantages for third-party add-on expense reporting software. Our system identifies the department, customer, function and etc. We mainly concentrate on two things. i.e., Daily Expenses and Monthly Expenses.


Daily Expenses

In Daily Expenses section, we maintain records of the daily expenses such as couriers, paper usage, eatables, water, clothes, footwear etc.

Monthly Expenses

Here we maintain the monthly expenses records of any particular business or organization like Salaries, Rents, Bills, Telephone, Cable TV and Internet, Restaurants etc.

Important Aspects of Expenses

  • Time-Saving on Expense Reports: Maintenance of the employee expenses like office supplies, expenditure etc is not an easy task. We need to access all the reports and validate them. All these can be done with the help of our software.
  • Easy upload of the receipts: Here employees can easily attach the receipts of the expenses in the expenses record.
  • Team Expenditure: Concentrate on the expense records of your team and to ensure the costs within the budget.
