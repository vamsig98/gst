The manufacturing business aims to optimize the sales efficiently. The ERP sales process helps you to maintain the customer information and sales management take the complete responsibility for making sales and maintains the distribution process to gain profits.

ERP Sales implement order placements, scheduling the order, order shipping, as well as invoicing. This process makes the sales order, quotation, sales invoice, delivery note, debit note, etc. The sales order can be made against the quotation of sales manually. The two-level authorization functionality is available in this sales process which is used to maintain the rates and quantity respectively and that are easily traceable.


Sales Reports

Sales Reports are summarized daily, weekly, monthly, and early wise. These sales reports may give you the clear information on most popular products or an item which gives you the most revenue. The various sales reports give both the summary and your product sales. These daily, weekly, monthly, and early wise reports, you can know about the sales that are done in different months or weeks.
