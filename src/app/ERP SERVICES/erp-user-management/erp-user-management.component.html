We GstGola provides the best user management feature which helps in creating and as well as changing the username and passwords. With the help of this user management, you can create various types of users and also assign the authorization roles for controlling the applications.

This level offers the possibility of defining the various levels to access the information of a different group of users. Here are the types of users that are classified as follows according to our GstGola ERP Services. Our ERP Service consists of Super Admin, Admin, and Users. All the users or employees data is controlled by the super admin.


Users

They does not have any authorization rights in starting the sessions and accessing the data of a particular organization or company.

Admin

These people have all the authorization authorities and can create a number of users.

Super Admin

One who maintains the number of admins and this super admin is the head for all the admins who have the complete data of the employees or users.
